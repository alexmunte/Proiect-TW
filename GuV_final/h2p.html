Game instruction




Welcome to GuV!

           The purpose of the game is to guess which celebrity is in the immage. First celebrities will be easyer to guess, then it will get harder and harder. Under the picturesthere will be a blank box where the player will have to fill the name of the celebrity. If he can't guess it, he will have the possibility to use a "skip-button" which will get him to the next picture.
